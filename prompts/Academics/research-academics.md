### Chapters Overview:

1. **Introduction** ‚Äì Context, problem, purpose, questions, scope, significance  
2. **Literature Review** ‚Äì Theories, existing studies, frameworks, gaps  
3. **Methodology** ‚Äì Approach, design, sampling, tools, data process, ethics  
4. **Findings** ‚Äì Key results, representations, hypotheses, surprises  
5. **Discussion** ‚Äì Interpretation, relation to literature, limitations, impacts  
6. **Conclusion** ‚Äì Summary, contributions, future work, recommendations  
7. **References** ‚Äì Proper citations for all used sources
